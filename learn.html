Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about event planning and our services

How far in advance should I book event vendors?

Booking timelines vary by vendor type and event size. For popular vendors (venues, photographers, caterers), we recommend booking 9-12 months in advance for weddings and 6-9 months for corporate events. For other vendors, 4-6 months is typically sufficient. High-demand dates (summer weekends, holidays) may require even earlier booking.

What is included in your full-service event planning package?

Our full-service package includes comprehensive planning from concept to execution: venue selection, vendor management, budget creation and tracking, timeline development, design conceptualization, day-of coordination, and post-event evaluation. We handle all logistics and communications, allowing you to enjoy the event without stress.

How do you handle last-minute changes or emergencies?

Our team is trained to handle unexpected situations with professionalism. We maintain detailed contingency plans for common issues (weather, vendor cancellations, etc.) and have established relationships with backup vendors. On event day, our team includes a dedicated problem-solving manager to address any issues immediately and discreetly.

Can you work within my specific budget constraints?

Absolutely. We specialize in creating exceptional events at various budget levels. After understanding your priorities, we'll allocate resources strategically, suggest cost-saving alternatives, and leverage our vendor relationships to maximize value. We're transparent about costs and will provide options at different price points for each element.

How do you incorporate sustainability into events?

We prioritize sustainability through: digital invitations, locally-sourced catering, reusable decor, waste reduction plans, carbon offset programs, and ethical vendor selection. We'll develop a customized sustainability plan for your event with measurable goals and can provide reporting on your event's environmental impact.
