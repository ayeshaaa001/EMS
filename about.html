Our Story

Founded in 2015, MULAQAAT began as a small team of passionate event enthusiasts in Jhelum. What started as a dream to create memorable gatherings has grown into a premier event planning company serving clients across Pakistan.

"Our mission is to transform ordinary events into extraordinary experiences through creativity, attention to detail, and flawless execution."

Over the years, we've had the privilege of orchestrating over 2k+ events, from grand weddings to large corporate gatherings. Our journey has been fueled by our commitment to excellence and the smiles on our clients' faces.
