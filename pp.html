Cancellation Policy

We understand that plans can change. Our cancellation policy is designed to be fair to both event organizers and attendees.

For Event Attendees

  • Standard Events: Cancellations made 72 hours or more before the event receive a full refund. Cancellations within 72 hours are non-refundable.
  • Premium Events: Cancellations made 7 days or more before the event receive a full refund. Cancellations within 7 days receive a 50% refund.
  • Digital Events: No refunds once access has been provided.

For Event Organizers

If you need to cancel or reschedule your event:

  • Notify us at least 14 days before the event date for a full refund of our service fees
  • Cancellations between 7-14 days before the event receive a 50% refund
  • Cancellations within 7 days are non-refundable

In case of events canceled due to unforeseen circumstances (natural disasters, pandemics, etc.), we will work with you to reschedule or provide credit for future events.

How to Cancel

To cancel an event or registration:

  1. Log in to your MULAQAAT account
  2. Navigate to "My Events" or "My Registrations"
  3. Select the event and choose "Cancel"
  4. Follow the on-screen instructions

For assistance, contact our support team at support@mulaqaat.com or call +92 313-4582342.

Sustainability Commitment

At MULAQAAT, we believe in creating memorable events while minimizing our environmental impact. Our sustainability commitment guides every aspect of our operations.

Our Environmental Pledge

  • Reduce carbon emissions from our events by 50% by 2030
  • Achieve zero waste to landfill for our operations by 2028
  • Source 100% renewable energy for our offices by 2026
  • Plant 10,000 trees annually through our reforestation program

Green Event Practices

We encourage and support sustainable event planning through:

  • Digital Solutions: Promoting paperless ticketing and digital event materials
  • Sustainable Venues: Prioritizing venues with green certifications
  • Waste Reduction: Implementing comprehensive recycling and composting programs
  • Local Sourcing: Supporting local vendors and sustainable catering options
  • Carbon Offsetting: Offering carbon offset options for event travel

We're proud to be certified Carbon Neutral since 2023 and are actively working toward becoming a Certified B Corporation.

Partnerships for Change

We collaborate with environmental organizations including:

  • Green Events International
  • Sustainable Events Alliance
  • Global Reforestation Initiative
  • Clean Oceans Project

Our Progress

Since launching our sustainability program in 2020:

  • Reduced paper usage by 92% through digital solutions
  • Diverted 85% of event waste from landfills
  • Offset 1,200 tons of CO2 emissions
  • Supported 15 local environmental initiatives

We're committed to transparent reporting on our sustainability journey. View our annual Sustainability Report on our website.
