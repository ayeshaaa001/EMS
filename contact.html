Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my event?

We recommend booking at least 3-6 months in advance for weddings and large events, and 1-3 months for smaller gatherings. Popular dates fill up quickly!

Do you provide catering services?

Yes, we offer comprehensive catering services with customizable menus to suit any dietary requirements or cultural preferences.

What areas do you serve?

We serve the entire Punjab region with our main focus on Jhelum and surrounding cities. We also travel nationally for large events.

Can I customize my event package?

Absolutely! We offer fully customizable packages to match your specific needs, preferences, and budget.

How do payments work?

We require a 30% deposit to secure your date, with the balance due 14 days before your event. Flexible payment plans available.

What safety measures do you have?

We strictly adhere to all local safety regulations, provide trained security staff, and implement comprehensive safety plans for every event.
